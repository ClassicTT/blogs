Top 10 Cryptocurrencies Leveraging DAG Technology

While blockchain remains the backbone of most cryptocurrencies, an alternative architecture called Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) is gaining traction for its efficiency and scalability. Unlike traditional blockchains that rely on miners and sequential blocks, DAG operates on a web-like structure where transactions validate each other. This design significantly improves transaction speeds, reduces fees, and minimizes energy consumption, making it a compelling solution for modern distributed ledger needs.
